About Me

Hey! I’m Mark, a 29-year-old photographer from California, and my camera has taken me to some of the most breathtaking places on Earth. From the towering peaks of the Swiss Alps to the vibrant streets of Tokyo, I find beauty in every corner of the world. My passion lies in capturing raw, unfiltered moments—whether it's the golden sunset over the Santorini coastline or the quiet charm of a hidden Parisian café. I shoot with a Canon EOS R5, paired with my favorite 50mm prime lens for crisp portraits and a 16-35mm wide-angle lens for those sweeping landscape shots. Whether I’m photographing a solo traveler, a couple’s love story, or a breathtaking cityscape, my goal is always the same: to turn moments into memories. Where will my next adventure take me? Maybe to tell your story.
